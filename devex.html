The Developer Exchange Program, also known as DevEx, provides an opportunity to make money by creating fantastic experiences on Roblox. Once you accumulate 30,000 Robux or more, you become eligible to convert your virtual earnings into real-world cash. Participants in DevEx are allowed to submit one completed DevEx request per calendar month.

To utilize DevEx, you need to agree to its Terms of Use and fulfill the simple requirements stated therein. Some of these requirements include: .


  1. Having a minimum of 30,000 earned Robux in your account.
  2. Possessing a verified email address.
  3. Holding a valid DevEx portal account.
  4. Being 13 years of age or older.
  5. Being a community member in good standing and having complied with Roblox's Terms of Use.
